🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Who we are

Friends of Solway is a parent-led committee operating under the direction of the Solway Primary School Board of Trustees, Masterton, New Zealand. We can be contacted at office@solway.school.nz.

What information we collect

When you use our pizza lunch ordering service, we collect the following information:

  • Your name and email address
  • Your phone number
  • Your child's name and class
  • Your pizza selections and order history

Payment information (card details) is processed directly and securely by Stripe. Friends of Solway does not store or have access to your card details at any time.

How we use your information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Process and fulfil your pizza lunch orders
  • Send you order confirmations and receipts
  • Contact you if there is an issue with your order
  • Pre-fill your details for future orders to save you time

We do not use your information for marketing purposes, and we do not share your information with any third party other than Stripe (for payment processing) and Domino's (for order fulfilment).

Data storage

Your order information is stored securely in Google Sheets, accessible only to authorised Friends of Solway committee members. We retain order data for the current school year and delete it at the end of each year.

Your rights

Under the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020, you have the right to access the personal information we hold about you, and to ask us to correct it. To make a request, please contact us at office@solway.school.nz.

Cookies

Our website uses your email address stored in your browser's local storage to remember your details between visits. No tracking cookies or third-party analytics are used.

Contact us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your information, please contact us at office@solway.school.nz.
