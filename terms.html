The pizza lunch service

Our pizza lunch ordering service allows parents and caregivers of Solway Primary School students to pre-order and pay for pizza lunches to be delivered to school on Fridays. The service is operated by Friends of Solway in partnership with Domino's.

By placing an order, you confirm that:

  • You are a parent or caregiver of a currently enrolled Solway Primary School student
  • The details you provide (including your child's name and class) are accurate
  • You have read and accept our Refund & Cancellation Policy

Pricing and payment

All prices are displayed in New Zealand dollars and include GST. A small payment processing fee may be added at checkout to cover costs charged by our payment provider, Stripe.

Payment is taken at the time of ordering. Orders are not confirmed until payment has been successfully processed.

We use Stripe to securely process all card payments. Friends of Solway does not store your card details. By completing a payment, you also agree to Stripe's Terms of Service.

Order cutoffs and delivery

Orders close at 12pm on the Wednesday before each Friday delivery. Orders placed after this time will not be included in that week's delivery. Please refer to our Refund & Cancellation Policy for details on cancellations.
